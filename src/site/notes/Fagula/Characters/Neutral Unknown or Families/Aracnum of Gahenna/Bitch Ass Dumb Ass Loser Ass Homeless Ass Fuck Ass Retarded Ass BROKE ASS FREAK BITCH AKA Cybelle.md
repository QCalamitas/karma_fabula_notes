Cybelle
--
___

She is the mother of [[Fagula/Characters/Allies/Reemus, The Pawn.\|Reemus, The Pawn.]] and presumably a part of the [[Fagula/Things/Arcanum Sets/Chess Set Arcana\|Chess Set Arcana]].
